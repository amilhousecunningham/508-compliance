Question # 1 - What is 508 Compliance?

Answer: 508 Compliance came about after the Workforce Rehabilitation Act of 1973 was amended in 1998. Section 508 required that that electronic technologies, such as websites and other forms of multimedia, meet accessibility standards to ensure individuals with disabilities have access to digital platforms.


Question # 2 - Why is 508 Compliance important?

Answer: 508 Compliance is important because the guidelines ensure that individuals with disabilities have access to digital platforms. Also, 508 Compliance ensures that individuals, companies, and organizations are not sued for violating the Civil Rights Act, American Disabilities Act, and other related laws.


Question # 3 - What are some important considerations for making websites 508 compliant?

Answer: Some important considerations include image alt text, keyboard accessibility, semantic HTML structure, consistent navigation, color contrast, accessible hyperlinks, and more.
